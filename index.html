FC 24

EA Sports FC 24 is an association football-themed
simulation video game developed by EA Vancouver and EA
Romania and published by EA Sports.

Valorant

Valorant is a free-to-play first-person tactical hero shooter
developed and published by Riot Games, for Windows.
Teased under the codename Project A in October 2019, the
game began a closed beta period with limited access on
April 7, 2020, followed by a release on June 2, 2020.
